Project 1: Blind Navigation System

This project utilizes ultrasonic sensors and a NodeMCU to assist visually impaired individuals in walking safely.
It incorporates vibration motors attached to the user's leg, which provide feedback about obstacles in their path.

Project 2: Automated Medicine Dispenser

The automated medicine dispenser is controlled by a NodeMCU and can be managed by a guardian through their smartphone.
Users can set timers for medication dispensing, and the device emits beeps as a reminder.
